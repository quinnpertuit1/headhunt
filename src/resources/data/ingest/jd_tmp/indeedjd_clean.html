Software Designer
Benefits Data Trust (BDT) - Philadelphia, PA

Benefits Data Trust (BDT) is a national not-for-profit organization committed to transforming how individuals in need access public benefits. BDT has an outstanding opportunity for an experienced full-time Software Designer to join our team.

Our rapidly growing organization is looking to expand our in-house development team to meet the continued demand for technology innovation. BDT supports a collaborative environment and values teamwork. we strive for a healthy, productive work environment, and want our staff to enjoy the opportunity they have to substantially contribute to the direction and success of our organization.

Position Summary:
The Software Designer will be working on PRISM, the CRM and benefit estimation tool that powers our call center, and has empowered BDT’s benefit specialists to aid over 500,000 people to find the help they need.
Under the direction of the Lead Software Engineer, the Software Designer will assist him by creating prototypes and concepts of the next features of PRISM. Working experience designing browser-based software is required. The specific technologies and levels of proficiency used can vary; however, preference would be shown to candidates who are fluent in front-end languages, like HTML, CSS, and even JavaScript. Be prepared to show examples of your UX/UI work and explain how your designs have aided an application.
We are friendly and highly collaborative, so we want you to be too. We like reasonable, opinionated people and expect friendly sparring about best coding practices in meetings and around the office. All voices are heard, but loyalty to decisions is expected. Our working environment is fun, caring, and relaxed while our work is passionate, serious, and professional.
The Software Designer position is an opportunity to make a vast impact by simplifying the tasks of co-workers continually and by aiding the needs of people across the country systematically.
The Software Designer will report to the Lead Software Developer.

Primary Responsibilities:

  • Proactively seek out and complete designs of features in a reasonably scheduled time;.
  • Pair with developers, QA specialists, and stakeholders to troubleshoot and help/learn;
  • Collaborate with fellow team members to better our process.

Qualifications:

  • 1+ years of experience designing browser-based software applications;
  • Experience working with Ruby on Rails frame work or APIis preferred;
  • Experience in other aspects of web development like graphics, video, writing, server admin, database optimization, and or deployment automation is a plus.
  • Excellent problem solving and communication skills, a detail-oriented personality, a collaborative spirit, and a love for technology that will ultimately impact the lives of others;
  • College degree –especially in a design or CS-related field is a plus.

Benefits Data Trust is an equal opportunity employer. We offer a supportive work environment and excellent benefits. Interested candidates who meet the above qualifications are invited to submit a resume and cover letter to the given email address. No phone calls please. Applicants must be able to pass a criminal background check and drug screen and provide three professional references. Candidates must be U.S. residents and live in/near the Greater Philadelphia Area or willing to relocate.
Compensation: Salary will be commensurate with experience + comprehensive benefits package

To Apply: Please submit a copy of your portfolio, resume and cover letter with salary requirements.

Job Type: Full-time

Required experience:

  • Designing browser based software applications: 1 year

Required education:

  • Bachelor's
